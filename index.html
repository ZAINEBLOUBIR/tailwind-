kitchen classic

Classic kitchens are designed to be timeless, simple and fresh, rather than dark, complicated, and traditional. Sticking to a light colour scheme with occasional dark accents will make your life much easier when it comes to creating a modern classic kitchen design.

kitchen modern

Modern kitchen designs have open floor plans stretching the entire length of the kitchen layout. They sometimes have distinct zone divisions which are subtly defined by changes in colour or materials. This minimalistic approach to layout is what achieves a simple and elegant look.

kitchen traditional

Traditional kitchens are characterised by the use of natural materials, soft muted colours and decorative details such as raised panel cabinets and crown molding.
